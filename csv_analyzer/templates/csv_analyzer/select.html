CSV Analyzer

Quickly get an understanding of the data in a *.CSV file.

When you receive a CSV data file from a client or colleague, this tool will immediately give you answers to questions such as:

  • How many rows and columns does the file contain?
  • For each column:
    • What is the name and data type?
    • How many rows are missing data?
    • How many rows contain unique (as opposed to repeating) values?
    • What are the minimum, maximum, mean and most frequent values?

In the time it takes the results page to load, the *.csv file you select will be opened, read, and analyzed. Give it a try!
